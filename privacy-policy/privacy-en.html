Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 16, 2026
This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile games provided by DodoSoft ("we," "us," or "our"), including Neon Drop, BattleCandy, and any future titles (collectively, the "Services").

We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using our Services, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (AAID) on Android, Apple Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) on iOS
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, system language, country code, screen resolution
  • App Usage Data: Play time, in-game events (level progress, game over, item usage), app version
  • Crash Logs and Diagnostics: Crash reports, error logs, performance data
  • Approximate Location: Country-level location derived from IP address (for advertising purposes only; no precise location is collected)

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as names, phone numbers, postal addresses, or account credentials.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide advertising services and display relevant ads
  • To improve our Services and user experience
  • To fix bugs and maintain app stability
  • To analyze usage statistics

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain collected information only as long as necessary to provide the Services. Specifically:

  • Game records (high scores, etc.) are stored locally on your device (PlayerPrefs) and are deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Advertising data is handled according to Google AdMob's policies
  • Crash logs are retained for up to 90 days

When you uninstall the app or request deletion, your information will be deleted without undue delay.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent for personalized advertising (via in-app settings or device settings)

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at the address provided below.

How to Reset Your Advertising ID

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will promptly delete the information.

7. European (EEA/UK) and California Users

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access, rectification, and erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing
  • Right not to be subject to automated decision-making

We use Google UMP to manage your consent for personalized advertising. You may withdraw or modify consent at any time through the in-app settings.

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights
